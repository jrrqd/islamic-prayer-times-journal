Welcome to Prayer Times

Assalamu alaikum! Thank you for installing Prayer Times. We're excited to help you stay connected with your daily prayers through this thoughtfully designed application.

Key Features

🕌 Accurate Prayer Times

Get precise prayer times based on your current location. The app automatically detects your location and displays prayer times for Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, and Isha.

⏱️ Real-Time Countdown

Stay informed with a live countdown to the next prayer, complete with a visual progress bar that shows how much time remains until the next prayer.

📝 Todo List

Keep track of your daily tasks with the built-in todo list. Add, check off, and manage your tasks easily. Your todos sync across devices when you're signed in.

📑 Virtual Desktop Pages

Organize your workflow with two virtual desktop pages:

  • Page 1: Prayer times, countdown, and todo list for daily planning
  • Page 2: Personal journal and reflection space

Switch between pages using the ← and → arrow keys or the dot navigation at the bottom.

📔 Journaling

Document your spiritual journey and daily reflections in the built-in journal (Page 2). Features include:

  • Daily journal entries with auto-save
  • Reflection prompts for spiritual growth
  • Private and secure - your entries stay on your device
  • Format text with markdown for better organization

🖼️ Beautiful Backgrounds

Enjoy automatically updating beautiful nature backgrounds that create a peaceful atmosphere while you check prayer times.

📖 Daily Islamic Proverbs

Get inspired by randomly selected Islamic proverbs that change with each new tab.

🔄 Easy Updates

Simply click on your location name to refresh prayer times whenever needed.

Pro Tips

  • Use arrow keys to quickly switch between pages
  • Click your location name to manually refresh prayer times
  • The progress bar shows how far along you are between prayers
  • Prayer times are automatically cached for offline access
  • Todo list items and journal entries sync across all your devices
  • Double-click the journal area to start writing

Thank you for choosing Prayer Times. We hope this app helps you maintain your daily prayers and brings baraka to your life.
