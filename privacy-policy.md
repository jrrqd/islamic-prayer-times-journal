# Privacy Policy for Prayer Times Chrome Extension

Last Updated: [Current Date]

## Introduction
Welcome to the Prayer Times Chrome Extension privacy policy. This document outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our extension.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
- **What we collect**: Geographic location (latitude and longitude)
- **How we collect it**: Through browser's geolocation API with your explicit permission
- **Why we need it**: To calculate accurate prayer times for your location
- **How we use it**: Only for prayer time calculations; never shared with third parties

### Prayer Times Data
- **What we collect**: Calculated prayer times based on your location
- **How we store it**: Locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API
- **Duration**: Stored until you clear your browser data or uninstall the extension

### Todo List Data
- **What we collect**: Todo items you create
- **How we store it**: Locally in your browser
- **Access**: Only you can access your todo list
- **Duration**: Persists until you delete items or uninstall the extension

### Google Calendar Integration
- **What we access**: Your Google Calendar when you choose to add prayer times
- **How we use it**: Only to create prayer time events upon your request
- **Authentication**: Uses Google's secure OAuth 2.0 protocol
- **Scope**: Limited to creating calendar events only
- **Duration**: Access token is temporary and only used when adding events

## Data Storage and Security
- All data is stored locally on your device
- No data is transmitted to our servers
- We use Chrome's secure storage APIs
- Calendar integration uses Google's secure authentication

## Third-Party Services

### Google Calendar
- Used only when you choose to add prayer times to your calendar
- Subject to Google's privacy policy
- We don't store your Google credentials

### Background Images
- Images are fetched from Bing/Unsplash
- No personal data is shared with these services
- Image loading follows their respective privacy policies

## Your Rights
You have the right to:
- Deny location access
- Clear stored data through Chrome settings
- Uninstall the extension at any time
- Request information about your stored data

## Data Protection
We implement security measures to protect your data:
- Local storage only
- No external data transmission except for essential services
- Secure API implementations
- Regular security updates
